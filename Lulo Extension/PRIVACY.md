# Privacy Policy for Lulo Chrome Extension

**Last Updated: December 5, 2025**

## Overview

Lulo ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Lulo Chrome Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Task Queries**: When you type a task or question, it is sent to our AI service (Google Gemini) to generate a response.
- **Page Context**: When you use browser automation features, the extension may access the current page URL and visible content to help complete your task.

### What We Don't Collect
- We do not collect or store your browsing history
- We do not track your activity across websites
- We do not collect personal identification information
- We do not use cookies for tracking purposes

## Data Usage

- Task queries are processed by Google Gemini AI to generate helpful responses
- Page content is only accessed when you explicitly request browser automation
- No data is sold to third parties
- No data is used for advertising purposes

## Data Storage

- The extension stores your preferences locally in Chrome's storage
- Conversation history is kept only in your current browser session
- No data is permanently stored on our servers

## Third-Party Services

The extension uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Gemini AI**: For processing AI requests (subject to Google's privacy policy)

## Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:
- `activeTab`: To interact with the current page when you request automation
- `sidePanel`: To display the chat interface
- `storage`: To save your preferences locally
- `tabs`: To get information about the current page
- `scripting`: To execute automation commands on pages

## Your Rights

You can:
- Uninstall the extension at any time to stop all data processing
- Clear local storage through Chrome's extension settings
